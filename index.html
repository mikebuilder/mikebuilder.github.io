Hi, I'm Michael.

I'm a fourth year undergraduate student currently attending Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), where I am completing a Bachelor of Science with a double major in Robotics Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. WPI's project-based learning curriculum has allowed my to thrive and develop real-world problem solving skills by applying what we learn in class to various projects that bring the theoretical concepts to life.

Outside of regular class work my favorite activity is the Formula SAE team, where we build a formula-style racecar to compete in an international design and race competition. This year I am leading the design of an all-new gear shifting system, using a geared servo to replace our old solenoid actuator, to have faster, more reliable gear shifts

In my free time, I love skiing, sailing, windsurfing, and just about any other watersport. My family has a house on Cape Cod, where we windsurf, and sail our Hobie 16 catamaran, and our favorite ski spot is Cannon Mountain in New Hampshire.
